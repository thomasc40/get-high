Illegal drug use causes about a quarter of a million deaths per year, according to the report, and use is highest in developed countries. The report is the first in a series of papers published in the journal the Lancet that address the burden of disease due to illicit drug use. It reviews information on the prevalence of illegal drug use, as well as its acute and chronic health effects.
